This plot shows how important the main characters are in four sitcoms. Importance is measured by their betweenness centrality in their respective character networks. These four series have around 5 main characters, their stories take place in a large American city and are all sitcoms. Seinfeld, HIMYM, Big Bang Theory all have main characters (Jerry, Ted and Sheldon), while in Friends all 6 have the same importance in the network.
